About Me

Hello! I'm **Humaira Kousar**, a **PhD Student** in the School of Electrical Engineering at **KAIST**, based in **Daejeon, South Korea**. My academic journey began with a deep fascination for **Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning**, particularly in areas like **Active Learning** and **Distributed Machine Learning**. Since then, I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects, from **expediting split learning** to developing **pruning-based data selection and network fusion**, collaborating with **Prof. Jaekyun Moon's lab**.

I believe in **innovative problem-solving** and contributing to advancements in the field of machine learning. When I'm not immersed in research, you can find me engaged in **social work** or exploring new places. I'm always eager to connect with fellow researchers and explore new opportunities in AI/ML.
